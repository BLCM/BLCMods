Turns the Probe into something even better: The Neuromancer.

[Notes]

Actually not useful on Aurelia due to fire explosion. You can disable that.
Best used on maelstrom Athena, if you have a shock Neuromancer. (Probably very OP on Maelstrom Athena)

[Features]

- Slightly increased damage, reload speed and clip size.
- Shots can ricochet once, and if they do create a bigger elemental explosion, matching your weapon element, if they hit something.
- Shots explode into a Fire and Explosive nova, along with a nova with the element your Neuromancer currently has.
- Not elemental locked!
- Burst fires 3 rounds with increased damage when aiming down sights.
- Damage still heals you, because it is a moxxi weapon.

[Where To Obtain]

Can be obtained from the Shock Drop Slaughter Pit when using the Community Patch.

[Changelog]

V1.0 Initial release.
