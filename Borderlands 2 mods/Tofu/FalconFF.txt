##  THIS GUN REPLACES THE LONGBOW ! ! !

##	Functionally, this rifle is similar to the Bird (obviously). Aiming down sights allows powershots to be fired, which discharge your entire mag at once.
##	Scoring critical hits in rapid succession will make powershots stronger and stronger. Yes, these bonuses do stack. You can keep track of your stacks
##	by looking at your mag size: more mag size = more powerful powershot. Stacks are on a timer and naturally decay after a while, eventually coming to a
##	stalling point.
##	Initial powershot damage is lower than a Bird powershot, however, if you are able to score a lot of critical hits the damage potential is
##	much higher. It gets pretty stupid. And louder.
##
##	Bore allows for multiple stacks to be proc'd with a single shot, especially on Loaders since their hitboxes can overlap.
##	Yes, the gun is meant to be used by Zer0, but I know people will use it with other characters anyway. I don't care. Have fun.
##	
##	There's probably a billion things I'm forgetting, but I'll let you figure those out on your own.
