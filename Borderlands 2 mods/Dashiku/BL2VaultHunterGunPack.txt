### Features all of my BL2 Vault Hunter themed guns - Note that this does NOT include my TPS themed guns ###

This is a pack that features my guns designed to represent BL2 Vault Hunters. It started with me making a Maya themed SMG and people saying they'd like to see more weapons like it. The guns are made to reflect the respective Vault Hunter's skills and abilties as best as i could create them. These guns will be a WIP in progress for some time still, but they are fully playable as they are. It is very much possible that they will get more features as i learn more about making these guns and certain technical limitations get lifted. More custom skins might also be a possibility in the future.

###################################################################################################################################################################################

### Axton ####
Crisis Management - Launcher - Replaces the 12 Pounder
 - Has 2 firing modes (hip-fire and zoomed).
 - Slow but high damage shots when hip-firing.
 - High DPS rapid-fire mode when zoomed.

Battlefront - Assault Rifle -  Replaces the Scorpio
 - Always deals explosive damage.
 - Increases max grenade capacity.
 - Shoots tiny nukes.
 - Counts as a non-elemental gun (works with Duty Calls).

### Maya #####
Ruin - SMG - Replaces the Chulainn
 - Shots deal equal parts Slag, Fire, Shock and Corrosive damage, in that order.
 - Fully elemental splash, no bullet damage.
 - Spawns healing trails when enemies are hit.

### Zer0 ####
Vel0city - Sniper - Replaces the Pitchfork - Has a custom skin made by Soze (Mike the Dutchman)
 - Shots pierce enemies.
 - Deals bonus melee damage on hit.
 - Bullets are near hit-scan.

### Salvador ####
Locked - Pistol - Replaces the Stalker (Had to replace the Stalker due to technical limitations, might change in the future)
 - Has a chance to add ammo to the clip when shooting, instead of using it.
 - Gains fire rate the longer you hold the trigger.
 - Overall increased stats.

Loaded - Pistol - Replaces the Grog Nozzle
 - Off hand pistol specifically made for Salvador.
 - Increased crit and melee damage.
 - Grants cooldown reduction and lifesteal when held.

### Gaige ####
Shock Storm - SMG - Replaces the Crit
 - High damage with low accuracy
 - Damage is focused on DoTs 
 - Shots can proc additional fire DoTs
!! When playing Gaige shots will ricochet towards enemies !!

### Krieg #####
Helfyre - Shotgun - Replaces the Jolly Roger
 - Deals massive fire damage
 - Increased melee damage (on par with the Rapier)
 - 100% ignite chance

## Changelog v1 ###
Many thanks to Lftstrafe for making videos on my weapons. Seeing the weapons in action and getting feedback in the comments has been a huge help and motivator. 

1. Made a uniform rarity list for my guns. Some of my guns were conflicting with each other. This should solve that issue.
2. All of the weapons now grant base ammo regeneration at 2/s.
3. All of the weapons now grant base 25% cooldown reduction.
4. All of the weapons now grant base 25% Movement speed.
5. Axton's Grenadier has been renamed to Battlefront. There was already an existing group of assault rifles in the game called Grenadier (Dahl with Torgue barrel). My weapon has been renamed to    avoid confusion.
6. Battlefront has slightly increased bullet speed.
7. Crisis Management has increased fire rate in barrage mode. Damage has been slightly reduced to compensate. This should make it match Scorched Earth a bit better.
8. Helfyre has been renamed to Fyre Feind. This was done to remove overlap with the Hellfire SMG.
9. Fyre Feind now replaces the Jolly Roger instead of the RokSalt. This saves me using the Octo's firingmode on the RokSalt. Trying to keep my footprint on the game as small as possible.
10. Vel0city now replaces the Pitchfork instead of the Sloth. Same idea as the Helfyre.
11. Vel0city gained a damage buff after feedback saying it was a bit too weak.
12. Vel0city SHOULD properly pierce nomad shields now.
13. Vel0city now has 3 ammo in the clip.
14. Locked has received a small damage boost.
15. Locked gains an additional 2/s ammo regen on top of the base.
16. Loaded gains an additional 25% cooldown reduction on top of the base.
