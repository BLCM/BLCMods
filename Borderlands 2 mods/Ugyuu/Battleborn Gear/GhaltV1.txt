Ghalt in Borderlands 2, AKA his UPR M8-R Revolver Shotgun.

Replaces the Triquetra.
Breaks nothing except for the fact that it makes the blockhead's splash always incendiary, which is really not a noticable change.
Not good with sanity check, so watch out with that to avoid your weapon from being removed.
I wanted to intentionally make this a strong shotgun while still consuming only 1 ammo per shot, so that it competes against the Interfacer and other shotguns.
"I'm like some kinda berserker.. but with guns. Wait i got it! Bergunzer!" -Ghalt

#</Notes>

#<Features>

Ghalt's skills as they are in the weapon itself.

The Hook: Ghalt fires out an energy hook that pulls enemies to him. --> Sadly, no changes.
Scraptrap: Deploys a trap to a target location which stuns enemies for a brief time, and then explodes, dealing damage. A limited number of traps can be deployed simultaneously. --> Sadly, no changes.
Dual Wield: Ghalt equips a second shotgun, doubling his damage output for a short time. --> Deals additional incendiary damage upon hitting an enemy. >>(This also refers to the final Helix upgrade Ghalt can have, in which incendiary shells are loaded.<<
Tactical Shells: The last shots in Ghalt’s Revolver Shotgun cylinder deal additional damage. --> Additional projectiles and damage..?
UPR-EC Technician’s Rig: Ghalt’s UPR engineering rig provides a decent amount of defense while also permitting heavy augmentation of Ghalt’s armament. --> Increases health by 25% while held and slowly regenerates shotgun ammo while held.

Aiming down sights activates the Revolver part of the UPR M8-R, making it shoot 1 very accurate projectile with high damage (equal to 10 when not aiming down sights) and increased fire rate.

[Additional Features]

- Heavily decreased fire rate.
- Semi-Automatic; No longer fires as fast as you can pull the trigger.
- Increased damage.
- Increased accuracy.
- Locked clip size of 8.
- Slower reload speed.

#</Features>
