The UPR-SL3 Tactical Rifle. Whiskey Foxtrot’s Tactical Rifle is accurate, deadly, and fires in three-round bursts.

    #<Features>

From which skills Whiskey Foxtrot represents.

Sticky Bomb: Hurls a grenade that sticks to surfaces and enemies. Detonates after several seconds, dealing damage to nearby targets. -> Shots deal bonus explosive damage.
Scrap Cannon: Fires a spread of shrapnel from the rifle’s underbarrel, dealing damage. >>>OPTIONAL:<<< Aiming down sights and shooting will release shotgun bursts of flak shots, causing reduced damage.
Overdrive: Loads Whiskey Foxtrot’s Tactical Rifle with a clip of bullets which can be fired fully automatically. -> Aiming downs sights allows the weapon to be fired fully automatically, dealing only slightly reduced damage.
Combat Rhythm: After each enemy kill, Whiskey Foxtrot’s attack damage is increased. -> At very unpredictable intervals, you will gain health regeneration, ammo regeneration and a big boost to gun damage.
UPR-RDC Combat Armor: Whiskey Foxtrot’s battered UPR armor still serves him well in battle – with a few modifications here and there, of course. -> Reduced damage from bullets.

- Burst-fires 3 rounds with each pull of the trigger.
- Increased damage.
- Clip size of 30. NOTE: It has been said that the base clip size is 24, but has not been confirmed.
- Increased fire rate.
- Decreased weapon swap speed.
- Increased accuracy by a large amount.
