Turns the Storm into a Railgun, controlled by a tiny penguin Battleborn. Don't call it cute.

[Note: Using vel0city or Accelerate will make some useful bits of the gun less useful. You can choose to use it or not.]

    #<Features>

From the skills that Toby has in Battleborn.

UPR H8-MS Custom Railgun: Toby's powerful Railgun can be charged, dealing increased damage at higher charge levels. -> This current weapon with modifications.
Arc Mine: Launches an Arc Mine, which arms after a brief delay, dealing area damage over time. Afterwards, Arc Mine will explode for additional damage. -> Storm shock explosions resemble the Arc Mine and deals damage to nearby enemies.
Force Field: Deploys an energy field that blocks enemy damage. Firing a Railgun shot through the field increases projectile velocity. -> You reflect bullets and take 15% less damage from those bullets. Your railgun shot explodes and accelerates after a very short while.
Boosters: Double-jumping while holding a direction grants Toby a quick boost in that direction. -> Slight speed and jump height increase while held.
Me 'N My Mech: Clad in his battle mech "Berg", the adorable-yet-fearsome Toby's considerable engineering talent provides for on-the-fly modification to respond to a variety of battlefield conditions: +5% shield capacity. Reason: Cuteness.
Core Discharge: Transform your mech to charge up a powerful laser that deals continuous damage over a period of time. -> Sorry! This cannot be done very well in Borderlands 2, so it's not a feature.

[Additional Features]

- Increased damage.
- Consumes 2 ammo per shot due to it being a powerful railgun.
- Slowly regens sniper ammo while held.
- Increased accuracy.
- Fires like a jakobs sniper with lower fire rate. Pump-Action-ish.
- While accelerating, the railgun shoots out shock orbs.
- Increased critical hit damage.
