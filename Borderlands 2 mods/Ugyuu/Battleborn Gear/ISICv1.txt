ISIC's implementation into Borderlands 2, with his Charge Cannon. A weapon for the truly defensive tanks.

Replaces the Yellow Jacket.

[Features]

The features that represent ISIC, his skills and his main weaponry.

Rotating Wards: Raise protective wards that orbit ISIC for a short time, each blocking damage from enemy fire. Overcharged: Each ward blocks more damage. -> 30% boost to all shield stats while held.
Energy Aegis: ISIC's secondary attack deploys his Energy Aegis to block incoming damage. -> +25% damage reduction while held. Has a 50% chance to reflect bullets at 500% damage while aiming down sights.
Plasma Dash: ISIC charges to a target location while engulfed in plasma, dealing damage to enemies along the way. Overcharged: Deal more damage to hit enemies. -> Increased movement speed and elemental effect chance slightly.
Overcharge: Activating a skill while ISIC's Charge Cannon is fully charged empowers that skill with additional Overcharged effects, and consumers the charge. -> No changes.
Omega Strike: Toggle turret mode, replacing basic attacks with improved weapons. Overcharged: Gain overshield on activation -> No changes, though this can be a seperate idea.

[Additional Features]

- Greatly increased damage.
- Low fire rate.
- Increased accuracy slightly.
- Aiming down sights charges ISIC's Cannon, firing a powerful, slightly slower blast of energy at the cost of 3 ammo per shot, with lower fire rate.
- Increased speed of the Yellow Jacket projectiles.
- Lowered ammo consumption to 1 per shot, but also lowered the clip size to 30.
