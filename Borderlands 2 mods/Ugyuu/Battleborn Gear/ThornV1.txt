Kreshek

Thorn's devastating Ekkuni longbow can be augmented to increase damage, range, and accuracy. Ready and hold an arrow to charge it.

[Features]

The features that are based off of Thorn and her skills.

Volley: Fires a horizontal swath of arrows, each dealing damage. Volley arrows ricochet off of world terrain. -> Aiming down sights allows the Kreshek to shoot 5 arrows with increased damage and lowered fire rate. All arrows ricochet 10 times.
Wrath of the Wild: After a brief charging period, hurls a massive energy bomb that explodes, damaging and knocking enemies backward. -> After a long charging time (distance) each arrow fired will spawn an explosion.
Eldrid Vitality: Thorn carries no shield, relying instead on rapid health regeneration to survive in combat. -20% shield capacity, +30% max health and rapid health regeneration while held.
Blight: Summons a temporary field of corruption. Enemies caught within this field take damage over time. -> Additional corrosive damage can deal damage over time.
Nature's Judgement: Enemies hit with a charged arrow are cursed for several seconds. Skills and charged arrows deal additional damage to cursed targets. -> Deals additional corrosive damage.

[Additional Features]

- Regenerates sniper ammo quickly while held, since the in-game Kreshek has infinite arrows.
- Increased fire rate, reload speed and accuracy.
- Slightly improved view when aiming down sights.
- Locked clip size of 5.
- Spawns in non-elemental only.
