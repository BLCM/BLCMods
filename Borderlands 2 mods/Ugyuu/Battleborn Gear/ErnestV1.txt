UPR-G73 Grenade Launcher. Ernest’s trademark UPR Grenade Launcher launches explosive canisters that detonate on impact with an enemy, or after a short delay.

#</Title>

#<Features>

[Representing the skills Ernest has]
~
Direct Hit: Grenades that directly hit an enemy deal +30% damage. -> (NOTE: Lvl 1 in-game helix allows Explosive Charge to push enemies) Direct hits will send the enemy flying.
Detonator: Ernest’s Detonator can be used to detonate deployed Explosive Charges or switch between Power Egg modes. -> Detonates other skill implementations on impact, i guess.
Explosive Charge: Heave an explosive charge that sticks to the world and enemies. Detonates to deal damage upon activation of Ernest’s Detonator. -> Landscaper's mines now have a greatly improved proximity and explosive radius.
Power Egg: Deploys a beacon that imparts bonuses to allies in range. Attack Mode boosts attack speed, while Defense mode reduces damage taken. -> Damage reduction and moral support for you while held.
Mine Grid: Deploys a grid of mines that explode on contact with an enemy, each dealing damage. Spawns arcing explosive shots on impact with anything!

[Further features]

- Massively increased damage
- Reduced reload speed and clip size, now holds 6 bullets.
- Very slow shotgun ammo regen.
- Greatly increased accuracy.
- Shots are now arcing grenades that explode on impact.
- Increased fire rate.

#</Features>
