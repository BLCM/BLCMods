				<comment></comment>

				<comment></comment>
				<comment>This mod only changes the APPEARANCE of weapons, no actual stats are affected</comment>
				<comment></comment>
				<comment>About green and blue bodies :</comment>
				<comment>By default, this mod only changes pistols (not Tediore ones) and shotguns bodies.</comment>
				<comment>The other weapon types are named as "Not recommended",</comment>
				<comment>not because it will break things, but because it doesn't look good.</comment>
				<comment>Image here : https://i.imgur.com/WHq2sdp.jpg</comment>
				<comment></comment>
				<comment>About unique bodies :</comment>
				<comment>Some weapons have unique bodies, that means we can change them !</comment>
				<comment>By default, this mod changes the body of some weapons so it matches their rarity.</comment>
				<comment>But you can put whatever body variation that you want, or leave unchecked for default look.</comment>
				<comment>Image comparison here : https://imgur.com/a/mgewZSD</comment>
