#<Legendarify>

    "And they will tell legends about us..."

    By Koby

    Changes the Rarity of many previously Unique (Aftermarket) Weapons and Items to Legendary.

    This mod doesn't change the Weapons and Items itself, only the Rarity color.

    Full Filter Tool support!

    Pick either the default option or the compatibility fix for my Rarity Colors Fix mod.

    If you use the Rarity Colors Fix Option, run this mod AFTER Rarity Colors Fix.

    DISCLAIMER:

    This mod will most likely conflict with any mod which changes rarities and/or rarity colors.
