#<Maliwan Brand Tweaks 1.1>

    All Maliwan products excluding Sniper Rifles receive quality of life changes or in the case of rocket launchers, a substantial redesign.

    Coded by Orudeon. It is highly recommended you use LightChaosman's Filter Tool to install this.

    NOTE: This mod is incompatible with other mods that alter Rocket Launcher barrels. Please read instructions carefully.

    Changelog: 

    V1.1:

    Updates the Pyrophobia to improve usability (hopefully)    

    V1.0a:

    Improved Maliwan pistol fire rate and clip size.
