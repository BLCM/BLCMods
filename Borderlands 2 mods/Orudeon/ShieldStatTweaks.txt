#<Shield Stat Tweaks>

#<Description>

Tweaks shield parts to bring the average quality of randomly generated shields up. In designing this mod, several core tenets were adhered to...
1) Don't nerf excellent attributes.
2) Buff terrible attributes to a minimum.
3) Buff underutilized brands so every brand has something going for it.

In general, if an attribute was increased, it may have been increased a lot. If an attribute was decreased, it probably wasn't decreased by much. Excellent brands were already very close to the target 'quality'. Overall, shields should offer a little bit more tankiness and special effects should be stronger.

By Orudeon, with assistance from SirUmnei, Zububu, AngrierPat, et al.

Please note that you must pick a Torgue capacitor in the Torgue section or this mod will default to the UCP version.

Due to an as of yet unfixable bug, the Cracked Sash's inventory presentation is broken by this mod. This makes it impossible to determine its recharge rate and delay without guesswork. Its HUD capacity displays correctly.

V1.2:
- Added compatibility for the UCP's Torgue capacitor buff.
V1.1:
- Fixed elemental immunity capacitors incorrectly not granting immunity/resistance.

#</Description>
