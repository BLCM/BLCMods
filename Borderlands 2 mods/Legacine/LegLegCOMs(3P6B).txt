I am grateful to SirUmnei, VulpesDolosus and Exotek for their teachings on creating custom class mods ("COMs").

Kindly note:
a. COM manufacturers are altered through Gibbed.
b. Each COM in this file grants three (3) passives and an additional five (5) skill points to six (6) skills ("3P6B").
c. My COMs are best used in conjunction with SirUmnei's COM Presentation Overrides list.
d. My COMs replace all the orange - rarity (i.e. Legendary) class mods of Borderlands 2 except for the Slayer of Terramorphous.
e. The Borderlands 2 GitHub and Shadow's Evil Hideout on Discord are the only authorized locations for downloading my COMs.
f. These COMs were designed with theNocturni's "UVHM+" series of mods in mind.
