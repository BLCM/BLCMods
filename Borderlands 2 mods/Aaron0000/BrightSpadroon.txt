#<BrightSpadroon>

    #<Description>

        By Aaron0000

        Turns the Crit into a recreation of the Pre-Sequel's Bright Spadroon.

        Features;

        High damage (no continuous firing bonus) and fire rate, massive magazine size, faster reload speed, lowered chance of causing status effects.

        Minimal range.

        High chance to reflect bullets.

        Notes;

        Disables Accelerate and Vel0city while active. This is done to prevent the beam from becoming longer.

        Does not spawn with the accelerated part, but doesn't delete said versions.

        Non-shock Crits will be deleted if you don't run this mod.

        Uses the firing mode of the Orc's secondary projectiles. Use the fix at the bottom to patch up the Orc. Also be wary of mods that use that firing mode.

        Includes a fix for the Grog Nozzle's skin, since the Grog uses the Crit as a base.

    #</Description>
