#<ETech Pistols Remastered>

By Aaron0000

Features;
Overhauls the Darts into a more useful form, and replaces Spikers with a new type of weapon (Plasma Pistol). Also turns the Logan's Gun and Dahlminator into plasma pistols, and the Wanderlust into a hybrid of spiker and plasma pistol.

#<Extended Description>

PLASMA PISTOLS;
Fires a Plasma projectile.
Significantly increased damage.
Increased Magazine size.
Slower reload speed.
Costs three ammo per shot (four for Maliwan.)
Significantly increased chance of applying elemental effects.
Dahl Plasma Pistols burst-fire three out-of-sights.
Aiming down sights (for all Plasma Pistols) and firing will launch a powerful projectile that is multiple times stronger, but costs just as much ammo (Up to seven times for both).
After firing a "charged" shot, you will be unable to fire the gun for a few seconds (Dahl variants have a shorter delay).

Credit to Dave for letting me use his Singing Bird as inspiration for Plasma Pistols.

Darts (the homing ones) have been significantly changed.

DARTS:
Projectiles now explode on impact with any enemy and have great homing capabilities (behaves like Wanderlust.)
Significantly increased damage.
Solid Accuracy.
Solid Recoil.
Good Spread.
Boosted Magazine size.
Slower Reload Speed.
Increased chance of appplying elemental effects.
Increased projectile speed.
Costs two ammo per shot (three for Maliwan.)
Dahl Darts burst-fire three out-of-sights, and five-aiming-down-sights.

NOTES:
Technically, the "Charged" shots are (up-to) seven projectiles simply fired so fast, that they register as one projectile upon hitting something (kinda like shotguns.) Therefore, if you happen to see a spray of projectiles when using less accurate versions of the weapon or Anarchy, don't be surprised.
If using S&S Forever, Old Hyperion, Atlas, or any combo of them, there are compatibility patches for each at the bottom.

#</Extended Description>
