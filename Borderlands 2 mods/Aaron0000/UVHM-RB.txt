#<UVHM-RB>

    #<Description>

        By Aaron0000

        Features;

        Heavily reduces the health-sponginess of enemies (and health regen) to eliminate the need for slag. Purple weapons should generally be effective at level 72 as long as your build is decent.

        Adjusts enemy shields to warrant use of Shock more often.

        Slag no longer increases damage output by 200%/300%. Now only increases damage output a little while applying other debuffs to enemies. Lasts ten seconds and includes a damage over time.

        Enables Health-Scaling in the Overpowered Levels.

        Compatible with Koby's Level 1 UVHM Mod. Load this one after it.

        #<Adjusts certain enemies that had extra health/shield bonuses in UVHM to accomodate for the reduced damage output with slag, or otherwise make them less annoying to fight. Said enemies are;>

            Rabid Enemies have less health. Rabid Stalkers also now only move at Rabid Skag speed. (Rabid Skag - x5 (from x8), Rabid Stalker - x4 (from x5))

            Juggernauts/Orczerkers recieve a small health nerf. (x8 (from x10))

            Witch Doctors have nerfed health to accomodate the Slag Changes. (Badass Health is unchanged. Fire/Shock - x2.2 (from x5), Slag - x2.0 (from x4), Slow - x1.8 (from x4.66), Vampire - x1.8 (from x4))

            The Ghost Kings no longer resist Shock, Corrosive, and Slag damage, putting them in line with most skeletal enemies.

            Terramorphous has nerfed health to accomodate the Slag Changes. (x300 (from x1000))

            Vermivorous has nerfed health to make it less of a slog to kill it after spawning it in the higher OP levels. (x100 (from x200))

            Hyperius and his minions have nerfed health to accomodate the Slag Changes. (Hyperius - x23 (from x56), Minions - x10 (from x25.75))

            Master Gee and his worms has significantly nerfed health to make him less of a slog to fight. (Master Gee - x400 (from x3500), Worms - x3 (from x20))

            Pete the Invincible and the pipe-enemies have nerfed health to accomodate the Slag Changes. (x135 (from x338). Pipe-enemy health nerfs are negligible.)

            Voracidous/Chief Ngwatu have nerfed shields to make the fight more bearable. Voracidous/Chief Ngwatu/Savage Murderers have nerfed health to accomodate the Slag Changes. (Vora/Chief shield nerfed by 65%. Voracidous - x370 (from x225). Chief/Murderer health nerfs negligible.)

            Dexiduous has a massive health nerf to make it less of an eternity to fight. (x20000 (from x100000))

            Ancient Dragons of Destruction have nerfed health to accomodate the Slag Changes. (Blue - x100 (from x300), (Red - x50 (from x200), (Green - x70 (from x150), (Purple - x40 (from x100))

        #</Adjusts certain enemies that had extra health/shield bonuses in UVHM to accomodate for the reduced damage output with slag, or otherwise make them less annoying to fight. Said enemies are;>

        #<Adjusts the elemental damage bonuses/weaknesses to not be so stringent on matching bonuses to deal adequate damage. The bonuses and weaknesses are>

            #<Incendiary>

                Deals 1.3 times damage on flesh targets in Normal Vault Hunter mode, 1.45 in TVHM/UVHM.

                Deals 75% damage to shields in Normal Vault Hunter mode, 50% in TVHM/UVHM.

            #</Incendiary>

            #<Shock>

                Deals 1.5 times damage on shielded targets in Normal Vault Hunter mode, 2 in TVHM/UVHM.

            #</Shock>

            #<Corrosive>

                Deals 90% damage to flesh targets in Normal Vault Hunter Mode, 80% in TVHM/UVHM.

                Deals 75% damage to shields in Normal Vault Hunter mode, 50% in TVHM/UVHM.

            #</Corrosive>

            #<Explosive>

                Deals 78%~ damage to shields in Normal Vault Hunter Mode, 70% in TVHM/UVHM.

            #</Explosive>

            #<Slag>

                Increases damage dealt to enemies by 15%.

                Reduces enemy accuracy by 33%.

                Decreases enemy fire rate by 25%.

                Decreases enemy reload speed by 25%.

                Decreases enemy movement speed by 33%.

                Deals 90% damage to armored targets, regardless of playthrough.

                Deals 90% damage to shields in Normal Vault Hunter mode, 80% in TVHM/UVHM.

            #</Slag>

        #</Adjusts the elemental damage bonuses/weaknesses to not be so stringent on matching bonuses to deal adequate damage. The bonuses and weaknesses are>
