########
By Aaron0000

Enables Jakobs and Torgue Weapons to spawn with E-Tech variants.

NOTES;
If using the Splitters mod, activate this mod before Splitters.
The game will delete the weapons if you do not run the mod before loading a save file.
Due to Darts/Spikers/Splat Guns using exclusively splash damage, the Jakobs variants of those will deal explosive damage.
Due to Blasters not having splash damage, Torgue Blasters have had their ammo consumption reduced to one to compensate.
These E-Techs operate in the same way Hyperion E-Tech Pistols can; they spawn on typical purple variants of the weapon, meaning Gemstone variants are possible (exclduing launchers of course).
Also probably enables Jakobs Pepperboxes to spawn as a side-effect.
