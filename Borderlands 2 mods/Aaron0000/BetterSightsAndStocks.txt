#<Better Sights and Stocks>

    #<Description>

        By Aaron0000

        Changes sights and stocks to be a bit more interesting.

        Features;

        Sights now affect a weapon's stats. They also have different movement penalties when zoomed in. Sights with weaker zooms have less penalty.

        Stocks, Exhausts, and Pistol Sights now have a bonus for being used with their matching manufacturer;

        Bandit - Reduced weapon spread or increased accuracy.

        Dahl - +1 to burst-fire count.

        Hyperion - Slower accuracy loss, achieves max accuracy faster.

        Jakobs - Small multiplicative critical damage bonus.

        Maliwan - Increased DoT Damage and Chance.

        Tediore - Passive ammunition regeneration for the active weapon.

        Torgue - Small chance to ignore shields.

        Vladof - Small chance to fire twice, Spiniguns spin longer and reach max spin faster.

    #</Description>
